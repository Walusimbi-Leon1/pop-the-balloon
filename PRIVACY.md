# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 18, 2026

## 1. Overview

Pop the Balloon ("the Game") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Game Data
- Display name (chosen by you or from Discord)
- Game responses and votes (anonymous during gameplay)
- Balloon color and game session info
- Match results

### 2.2 Discord Data (when used as Discord Activity)
- Discord username
- Discord avatar URL
- Discord user ID (for session management)

### 2.3 Technical Data
- Browser type and version (for compatibility)
- Session timestamps

## 3. How We Use Data

- To provide and maintain the Game
- To enable real-time multiplayer gameplay
- To display leaderboards and match results
- To improve the Game experience

## 4. Data Storage

Game data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database. Data is retained for the duration of the game session and may be cleaned up after 30 days of inactivity.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:
- As required by law
- To protect the safety of users
- With your explicit consent

## 6. Anonymity

During gameplay, your answers and votes are anonymous. Your display name is only revealed:
- At the end of a game round (to matched players)
- To the host for game management purposes

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## 9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through our GitHub repository.
