# 🎈 Pop the Balloon

**Hold your balloon. Find your match. Don't get popped.**

A fast-paced social party game for 2–12 players, built as a Discord Activity. Answer fun prompts, vote to keep or pop other players' balloons, and discover who your best matches are!

---

## 🎮 How to Play

### The Basics
Every player starts with a **balloon** and a name tag. Each round, a fun prompt appears on screen — things like:

- *"What's your most controversial food opinion?"*
- *"What's a hill you'll die on?"*
- *"What's your hidden talent?"*

Everyone types an **anonymous answer** and submits it. Then the answers appear one by one — and here's where it gets interesting.

### 💚 Keep or 🔴 Pop?

After each answer is revealed, **every other player** votes:

- **💚 Keep** — "I vibe with this answer!" The balloon stays.
- **🔴 Pop** — "Nah, that's wild." The balloon gets popped with a satisfying explosion! 💥

If your balloon survives all the rounds, you're a **match** with everyone who kept you. If you get popped... you're out!

### End of Game
After all rounds, the surviving balloons are revealed. Matched pairs (and groups!) are shown with a celebration screen. Play again to discover new connections!

---

## 🎲 Game Modes

### 🔵 Chill Mode
The classic experience. **5 rounds** of prompts with plenty of time to think and vote. Perfect for getting to know new people or hanging out with friends. Relaxed pace, maximum vibes.

### 🔴 Rapid Fire Mode
**10 rounds** with a **10-second timer** per vote. No time to overthink — go with your gut! Fast answers, split-second pops, and a chaotic energy that makes every round unpredictable. Best for experienced players who want intensity.

### 🟡 Blind Mode
The same as Chill, but **player names are hidden** until the end! You're voting purely on the answers themselves — no biases, no preconceptions. When the results reveal who wrote what, the surprises are *chef's kiss* 🤌.

## ✨ Features

- **🎯 Instant Multiplayer** — Join with one click inside Discord. No downloads, no installs, no accounts needed. Just open the Activity and play.
- **⚡ Real-time Sync** — All players see everything happen live. Votes, pops, and results sync instantly via Firebase Realtime Database.
- **🎈 Satisfying Animations** — Balloons float, pop with explosive effects, and confetti fills the screen on match reveals.
- **📱 Mobile Friendly** — Fully responsive design that works on desktop and mobile. Play from your phone inside Discord!
- **🔒 Anonymous Voting** — Answers are always anonymous until the end. Vote honestly without social pressure.
- **🎭 Three Game Modes** — Chill, Rapid Fire, and Blind keep the experience fresh and replayable.
- **👥 2–12 Players** — Small group hangout or full party. The game adapts to any group size.
- **💬 Conversation Starter** — Every prompt sparks genuine conversations. Perfect for icebreakers, dating servers, friend groups, or community events.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript — lightweight, fast, no framework overhead
- **Backend:** Firebase Realtime Database — real-time multiplayer sync with zero server setup
- **Platform:** Discord Embedded App SDK — native Discord integration, runs inside a call
- **Deployment:** Cloudflare Workers — global CDN, instant cold starts, zero maintenance

## 🚀 How to Play in Discord

1. Open a Discord voice or text channel

3. **Have fun!** The goal is connection and laughs, not competition.
